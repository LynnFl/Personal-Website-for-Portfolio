Project2

What do they eat?

In this game, players take on the role of an artificial intelligence system purchased by a school to oversee the operations of its cafeteria run by cater companies. The challenge lies in balancing profitability and smooth operations for the school and vendors while ensuring the health of students and controlling prices. Players will make decisions based on their own reasoning and judgment. In the game process, player can use a security camera operating system and a communication system to go with the storyline.
